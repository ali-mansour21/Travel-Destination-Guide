Lebanon

Nestled along the eastern shores of the Mediterranean Sea, Lebanon beckons travelers with its captivating blend of ancient history, vibrant culture, and breathtaking natural beauty. This small yet dynamic country boasts a rich tapestry of diverse landscapes, from the snow-capped peaks of the Lebanese mountains to the sun-kissed shores of its picturesque coastline.

Baalbek: Explore the ancient ruins of Baalbek, one of the most impressive Roman archaeological sites in the world. Marvel at the towering columns of the Temple of Jupiter, the grandeur of the Temple of Bacchus, and the intricate carvings that tell the story of Lebanon's rich history.
